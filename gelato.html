Italian Gelato

This classic gelato uses a custard base for a smooth and creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Heat milk and cream in a saucepan over medium heat until just beginning to simmer.

  2. Step 2

    Whisk egg yolks and sugar together in a bowl until pale and thick.

  3. Step 3

    Slowly pour the hot milk mixture into the yolks, whisking constantly.

  4. Step 4

    Return mixture to saucepan and cook, stirring, until it coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Step 5

    Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla and salt, then strain through a fine sieve.

  6. Step 6

    Chill thoroughly in the refrigerator.

  7. Step 7

    Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer directions, then freeze until firm.
